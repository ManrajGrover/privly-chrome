Markdown Tips

If you add the symbols shown below to the text you write, it will change how your text is displayed.

  • *Emphasis*
  • **Strong**
  • [link text here](link.address.here "link title here")
  • Line Break: a line ending in two spaces.
  • 1. An ordered list
  • * An unordered list
  • `code`

  • # heading

  • ## subheading

  • ### sub-subheading
